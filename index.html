About Me

I am Venkatesh S, a Machine Learning Engineer specializing in AI solutions for healthcare at ScriptChain Health. I am currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Science at The George Washington University, focusing on applying the latest advancements in AI and ML research, including the integration of large language models for accurate and timely data analysis.

At ScriptChain Health, I lead the development of AI models to predict patient readmissions. This involves researching and implementing cutting-edge techniques, handling temporal data, and leveraging AWS cloud technologies such as Bedrock and Athena for scalable model training and data management. I am passionate about using my skills to improve patient outcomes and drive innovation in healthcare analytics.

In addition to my work at ScriptChain Health, I contribute to the academic community as a Data Consultant at GW. I provide support to students, faculty, and staff on advanced statistical analysis using various software and programming languages like R, Python, and Tableau. I enjoy empowering others with data-driven insights and fostering a collaborative learning environment.

Prior to my current roles, I gained valuable experience as an RPA Developer at Tata Consultancy Services and further honed my skills during my internship at TCS iON. My diverse background has equipped me with a strong foundation in software development, data analysis, and cloud technologies. I am eager to continue contributing to the impactful intersection of AI and healthcare.
